About Our Mission

We believe every city can be transformed into a pet-friendly paradise

Creating Paw-sitive Change üêæ

Our Pet-Friendly City Campaign is a grassroots movement dedicated to transforming urban spaces into welcoming environments for pets and their owners. We work with local governments, businesses, and communities to implement pet-friendly policies and infrastructure.

From advocating for more dog parks and pet-friendly housing to organizing adoption events and educational workshops, we're committed to making cities more inclusive for our furry friends.
